  What is this website?

This website is a web application that allows you to import and store your Digital EU COVID certificates (DCC). Using your device's camera, you'll be able to scan the QR Code of the certificate.

Using the latest available technologies, this application can be installed on your smartphone, so you can use it even while offline.

The certificates are stored directly on your device, the application does not connect to any server during usage. Your data is safe and cannot be leaked. No cookies are stored and no third-party services are used.

The application can store an unlimited amount of certificates, if you need to keep the certificates of your family or friends.

Finally, this application is Open Source, the source is directly accessible and modifiable. Should you want it, you can audit it, and even contribute to improve it.

  What is a DCC?

An EU Digital COVID Certificate is a digital proof that a person has either:

  •   been vaccinated against COVID-19
  •   received a negative test result or
  •   recovered from COVID-19

Key features of the certificates are:

  •   Digital and/or paper format
  •   with QR code
  •   free of charge
  •   in national language and English
  •   safe and secure
  •   valid in all EU countries

More information is available on the European Commission website.

  Why this app?

In some countries, the official application to use for storing the certificate is bloated with other features, and sometimes even incompatible with some phones.
